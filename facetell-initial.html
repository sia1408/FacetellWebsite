Welcome to Facetell

Facetell is an advanced deep learning-based platform designed to detect manipulated media, particularly deepfake images and videos. With the rise of artificial intelligence tools capable of creating hyper-realistic media forgeries, the need for effective detection methods has become critical. Facetell is here to combat misinformation and protect the integrity of digital media.

At the heart of Facetell is the Xception model, a cutting-edge convolutional neural network (CNN) known for its ability to extract subtle features that indicate the authenticity of media. By leveraging a sophisticated deep learning architecture and transfer learning, Facetell is able to accurately differentiate between real and fake content, even in challenging scenarios.

Facetell is ideal for integration into social media platforms, messaging services, and other digital spaces to provide real-time verification of media authenticity, ensuring that misinformation can be curtailed at its source. Whether you are an individual user or an organization looking for reliable deepfake detection, Facetell offers a scalable, user-friendly solution.
